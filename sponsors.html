Partnership Programme · Season 2025–26

Invest in the next generation of engineers.

Spartan Racing is San José State University's Formula SAE team — a student-run engineering organization that designs, manufactures, and races open-wheel electric race cars. We compete against 600+ universities worldwide in the world's largest collegiate engineering competition.

Your sponsorship directly funds student hands-on engineering education: carbon fiber layups, PCB design, embedded firmware, powertrain integration, and aerodynamic development — all built from scratch by students.
